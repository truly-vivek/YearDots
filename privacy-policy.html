Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 26, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to YearDots, developed by Freak Codes ("we," "us," or "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This document explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use the YearDots Android application (the "App").

2. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

YearDots is designed to function as a live wallpaper on your device locally. It does not require you to create an account, does not track your location, and does not access your personal files, camera, or microphone.

3. Log Data and Crash Reports

In the event of an app error or crash, we may collect anonymous log data and crash reports through Google Play Services to help us identify and fix bugs. This data is strictly technical and may include:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol ("IP") address (anonymized)
  • Device name and operating system version
  • The time and date of the crash
  • App configuration at the time of the crash

This information is completely unidentifiable and is used solely to improve the performance and stability of the App.

4. Third-Party Services

The App is distributed through the Google Play Store and utilizes standard Google Play Services. These third-party services have their own Privacy Policies addressing how they handle data. We highly recommend reviewing the Google Play Services privacy policy for more information.

5. Security

We value your trust in downloading YearDots. Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data at risk of being breached from our end. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted.
